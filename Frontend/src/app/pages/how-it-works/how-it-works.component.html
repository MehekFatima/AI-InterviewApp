How It Works

Step 1: Enter Interview Details

Click on "Add New Interview" and fill in the job title, job description, and required experience.

Step 2: Grant Permissions

Once started, the app will ask for camera and microphone access. Grant the permissions to begin your mock interview.

Step 3: AI Asks Questions

Our AI will ask you 5 relevant questions based on the job description and title you provided.

Step 4: Get Feedback

After the interview ends, you will receive feedback on your responses to help you improve your skills.
